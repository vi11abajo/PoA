System Overview

The boost system in Pharos Invaders provides players with temporary or permanent upgrades that can dramatically change the course of the game. Boosts drop when destroying any enemy with a - probability.

To collect a boost, you need to touch it with Octo. After collection, the boost is instantly activated (for instant effects) or starts its action (for temporary effects).

Rarity Categories:

All boosts are divided into four rarity categories:

  • COMMON - basic effects with high drop chance, suitable for beginners
  • RARE - powerful balanced effects with medium drop chance
  • EPIC - game-changing effects with low drop chance
  • LEGENDARY - unique effects with special mechanics and extremely low drop chance

Boss Rewards:

Special Drop: Every boss defeated guarantees a Random Chaos boost drop with 100% probability at the boss location.
